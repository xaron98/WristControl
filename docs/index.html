Privacy Policy

WristControl  ·  Effective date: March 2026

WristControl does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Ever.

This privacy policy describes how WristControl (the "App") handles information when you use it on your Apple Watch, iPhone, or Mac. The short version is simple: no data leaves your devices, and no data is collected about you.

1. Information Collection

WristControl does not collect any information of any kind. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, account, etc.)
  • No usage data or analytics
  • No crash reports sent to external servers
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No location data
  • No health or fitness data

The App does not require you to create an account or log in.

2. How the App Works

WristControl allows you to control your Mac's display brightness and system volume from your Apple Watch. All communication happens exclusively between your own devices on your local network:

  • Your Apple Watch communicates with your iPhone using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework.
  • Your iPhone communicates with your Mac over your local Wi-Fi network using Bonjour (local service discovery) and a direct TCP connection.

No data ever leaves your local network. No external servers, cloud services, or relay servers are involved at any point.

3. Data Usage

Because no data is collected, there is no data to use, share, sell, or process. WristControl does not share any information with third parties under any circumstances.

4. Third-Party Services

WristControl contains no third-party SDKs, advertising libraries, analytics frameworks, or tracking tools. The App uses only Apple's first-party frameworks that are part of iOS, watchOS, and macOS.

5. Security

All communication between your devices takes place entirely within your local network. No data is transmitted over the internet. Because no personal data is collected or stored, there is no database of user information to protect or that could be compromised.

6. Children's Privacy

WristControl does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The App is suitable for all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date. Because the App collects no data, any changes would only arise from changes to the App's functionality.
